2. THE SERVICE

Our Platform connects riders who require taxi or cab services (“Riders”) with drivers who have a valid Public Passenger Vehicle (PPV) road license issued by the Transport Authority in Jamaica (“Drivers”). We provide an app-based service that enables Riders to hail rides from Drivers who operate PPV vehicles (“Vehicles”). Our services include matching Riders with Drivers, facilitating payment transactions between Riders and Drivers, and providing access to customer support. It is important to note that only Drivers who possess a valid PPV road license issued by the Transport Authority in Jamaica are permitted to provide services on our Platform. We take this requirement seriously as it ensures that our Platform operates in compliance with local regulations and promotes the safety and security of our Riders.

Our Platform provides a convenient and reliable way for Riders to access transportation services in Jamaica. By connecting Riders with PPV licensed Drivers, we ensure that our Riders are able to access safe and legal transportation options. Our app-based service allows Riders to easily hail rides from nearby Drivers, track their ride in real-time, and pay for their ride seamlessly through the Platform unless permitted otherwise.

We are committed to providing a high-quality and reliable service to both our Riders and Drivers. In addition to matching Riders with Drivers, we also facilitate payment transactions between Riders and Drivers to ensure a smooth and secure transaction. Our Platform also provides access to customer support for any questions or issues that may arise during a ride.

We recognize that the regulations in Jamaica may evolve over time. As such, we are committed to staying up to date with any changes and ensuring that our Platform operates in compliance with local laws and regulations.

10. FARE CALCULATION MANAGEMENT

10.1 The fare calculation management system uses a combination of distance traveled, waiting time, and base fare to determine the final fare for a ride.

10.2 Hitchhyke has the authority to set the maximum overcharge rate per unit distance, as well as the minimum per kilometer rate for all Drivers.

10.3 Drivers have the option to set their own per kilometer rate within the thresholds provided by Hitchhyke, choosing from a base price, a recommended price, or a maximum price.

10.4 The waiting period time increment will be determined solely by Hitchhyke at our discretion.

10.5 The final fare for a ride will be calculated based on the distance traveled and waiting time, using the Driver's selected per kilometer rate as a multiplier.

10.6 The fare calculation will take into account the base fare, distance, and waiting time. Toll costs will be managed through the User Input Method, which requires driver input and rider confirmation for accuracy.

10.7 User Input Method for Toll Calculation:

  • Pre-Pickup: Drivers enter anticipated toll fees, which are automatically approved and added to the fare. Riders receive a notification of these additions without requiring action.
  • Post-Pickup: Drivers prompt for toll entries after selecting "Drop Off Passenger." These added tolls require rider confirmation to be included in the final fare.

10.8 The fare, inclusive of the base fare, calculated distance, waiting time, and any confirmed tolls, as well as other surcharges, will be displayed to the Rider upon trip confirmation. This amount will be automatically deducted from their saved payment method at the end of a trip.

10.9 To ensure fairness and transparency, Hitchhyke will implement safeguards such as setting maximum and minimum toll amounts, limiting the number of tolls that can be entered per ride, and establishing a clear confirmation process for riders.

10.10 In instances where tolls are entered but not confirmed by the rider, or if the trip is ended before confirmation, those toll charges will not be applied to the trip.
