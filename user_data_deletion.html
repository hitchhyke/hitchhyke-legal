Connect / Disconnect Your Social Account

Connect a social account to your Hitchhyke profile for faster login. This is optional and can be done after signup or anytime while logged in.

This article covers supported accounts, how to connect, and how to disconnect.

How Social Login Works on Hitchhyke

  • All users must sign up with a phone number first.
  • After signup, you may connect a social account.
  • Once connected, use it to log in faster.
  • Connect or disconnect anytime from account settings.

Supported accounts:

  • Google (Gmail)
  • Facebook
  • Apple ID (Apple devices only)

How to Connect a Social Account

To connect:

  1. Open Hitchhyke app.
  1. Go to Main Menu.
  1. Tap Edit Profile or Account Settings.
  1. Tap the Connect Now button.
  1. Select your social account (Google, Facebook, or Apple).
  1. Follow authentication steps.

The account will appear as linked in your profile.

How to Disconnect a Social Account

Disconnecting removes Hitchhyke's access and prevents future login via that account.

Before disconnecting:

  • You must already be logged in.
  • Ensure you have access to your phone number or email to log in later.
  • Disconnecting does not delete your Hitchhyke account.
  • You can reconnect later.

To disconnect:

  1. Open Hitchhyke app.
  1. Go to Main Menu.
  1. Tap Edit Profile or Account Settings.
  1. Tap the Disconnect button.
  1. Confirm when prompted.

The account will be fully disconnected.

Data & Privacy Notes

  • Hitchhyke only accesses basic information required for authentication (as permitted by the provider).
  • Hitchhyke does not sell your personal data.
  • You can remove a social connection at any time.
  • For full account deletion, refer to Delete My Account in the Knowledge Base.

If you experience issues connecting or disconnecting a social account, contact Hitchhyke Support through the app.
